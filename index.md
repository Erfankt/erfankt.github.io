# Erfan Kefayat

### Urban Data Science 路 Geospatial Analytics 路 Policy Automation

I am a final-year **Ph.D. candidate in Public Policy Analysis** specializing in **geospatial data science**, **urban systems modeling**, and **AI-driven automation of local government workflows**.

My research focuses on developing **quantitative, scalable, and reproducible methods** to analyze urban form, infrastructure, and governance using spatialtemporal data. I work at the intersection of urban planning, data science, and public policy, with an emphasis on translating advanced analytics into practical decision-support tools for cities and public agencies.

### Research & Technical Focus
- Sustainable urban morphology and long-term spatial change
- Spatialtemporal modeling and clustering
- Street networks, land use, and built-environment analytics
- GeoAI and machine learning for urban systems
- Automation of zoning, permitting, and planning workflows

### Tools & Methods
